      Application Development

      Mobile, desktop, and other digital applications are ideal platforms for communication, processes, collaboration, and service delivery. Developing your own applications for customers or employees can drive value for key lines of business through purpose-built interfaces and functionality on these platforms. Applications can provide exceptional user experiences (UX) versus other methods as well.

      The Benefits of Application Development

      1

      STREAMLINE INTERNAL PROCESSES

      A custom application can streamline complex internal or customer-facing processes through a dedicated user interface (UI) designed with those processes in mind. Consolidate dozens of steps and tasks into a single environment so that customers or employees can drive results faster and with minimal friction. Enhance those processes with additional insights, collaboration, and features in a dedicated digital environment, ensuring the applications capabilities evolve with the needs of business.

      2

      ENHANCE THE USER EXPERIENCE (UX)

      A winning user experience will determine the success or failure of any digital initiative. Optimizing digital interactions via a dedicated application allows companies to isolate information, capabilities, and behaviors within a single, purpose-built environment, then enhance those aspects to the benefit of users and outcomes. Whether you represent a restaurant improving the ordering process for curbside pickup, or an enterprise improving employees experiences using analytics, an enhanced application UX takes you one step closer to realizing your applications full potential benefits.

      3

      OVERCOME UNIQUE MARKET CHALLENGES

      Organizations dont always have the right digital infrastructure to take on emerging market challenges in a timely and effective way. Leading companies use custom-built applications to overcome unique long-term or short-term market challenges, driving business continuity and success in unpredictable circumstances. A custom solution can make all the difference when use cases such as specialized alert systems, UIs for timely purchases, mobile apps for attendees at an industry event, and others emerge.

      4

      INCREASE VALUE

      A custom application provides all kinds of users with a destination to begin critical processes, functions, and requests, even when they have little initial information. Mobile apps that help consumers apply for mortgages, do their taxes, apply for jobs, and other tasks provide great value in terms of time and cost savings. Applications that help employees complete HR training requirements, apply for benefits, or automate key aspects of their responsibilities drive employee and business value as well.

      5

      DATA COLLECTION & ANALYSIS

      Applications centralize related functions and processes within a single environment, making them ideal for dedicated analytics associated with improving those functions and processes. Analytics aligned with those apps can provider organizations with great insights into user behaviors, industry trends, and customer sentiments as well. Thats because advanced analytics help technical employees analyze and optimize the application and the business in ways that are impossible in decentralized environments.

      6

      IMPROVE CUSTOMER ENGAGEMENT

      Customer-facing applications create all kinds of new opportunities for companies seeking to improve the ways they engage and interact with their customers. Implementation of special features like loyalty points, favorites, automated recommendations, and personalized discounts within the environment represent new opportunities for marketers to engage customers and improve their brand experiences.

      7

      BUILD CUSTOMER LOYALTY

      Custom applications can streamline the execution of loyalty programs, as well as expand the range of customer behaviors that can become part of companies loyalty initiatives. Easily adjust the offerings, point systems, and content associated with loyalty initiatives within the app environment. Companies can associate app access with loyalty features as well, incentivizing more users to download the app and maintain a connection via their mobile or other personal device.

      8

      DRIVE BUSINESS VALUE

      Not all applications succeed; but a carefully planned and executed application can drive real value in key areas of business. Companies who work with an application development expert can identify the right users, processes, features, and functions for their own successful digital application, ensuring their application use cases align with successful adoption and real ROI.

      What is Application Development?

      Application development is a series of processes that includes planning, creating, testing, and implementing dedicated digital, user-facing software applications for one or more mobile, desktop, tablet, or other devices. These programs, also called apps, are designed to centralize and optimize functions, processes, and user experiences associated with a single goal or set of goals, usually in line with specific business functions.

      1

      USE CASES FOR APPLICATIONS ARE GROWING

      As consumers and employees become more familiar with mobile and other digital applications, the market for application development continues to expand into more industries and use cases. There is a growing variety of application types and development approaches emerging as wellincluding custom, rapid, low-code, mobile, web, and enterprise application development, among otherseach of which serves unique purposes in the broader application development landscape.

      2

      LOW-CODE DEVELOPMENT IS BECOMING MORE COMMON

      Low-code or no-code application development involves the use of digital features to make application development easier for nontechnical users to executeeven among those with limited familiarity or abilities associated with traditional application development. Thats because low-code environments make it easier for users to take an intuitive approach to developmentincluding access to existing, pre-written code; drag and drop functionalities; and pre-built features they can easily configure for their organizations own unique use cases.

      Countless applications fail because companies do not have specific parameters in place to determine their success. Uvation helps companies identify the specific goals associated with application development and work to those ends in a holistic way, preventing pitfalls like feature creep, user confusion, unproductive workflows, and counterproductive backtracking or fixes.

For example, the challenges organizations seek to overcome with a new application are often temporary or fleetingthey require rapid development if an application fits at all.
