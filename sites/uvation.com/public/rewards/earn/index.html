      REWARDS: EARN FAQs

      Everything You Need to Know to Earn Points and Credits

      If you have anything else you want to ask, reach out to us.

      Can I earn points and credits on all purchases?

      There may be some exclusions on purchases that qualify for points and credits. Be sure to review the program terms and conditions for any limitations.

      Is there a limit to how many points and credits I can earn?

      There is no set limit on how many points and credits you can earn with Uvation Rewards. The more you engage with the program, the more rewards you can accumulate.

      What happens if I return an item I purchased using points or credits?

      If you return an item purchased with points or credits, the corresponding points or credits used for that purchase will be refunded to your account.

      How can I track my points and credits balance?

      Easily track your current Loyalty Points and USP Credits balance by logging into your Uvation Rewards account. Your balance will be clearly displayed on the dashboard.

      How long does it take for my points and credits to appear in my account?

      Loyalty Points and USP Credits typically appear in your account within 24 hours after completing a qualifying purchase or task.

      Do my points and credits expire?

      Yes, Loyalty Points and USP Credits do expire after a certain period of inactivity. You can find the specific expiration timeframe within the Uvation Rewards program terms and conditions.

      Is there a minimum purchase amount required to earn points or credits?

      Yes, there is a minimum purchase amount required to earn points and credits. The specific amount may vary depending on current promotions. You can find all the details on our website or app.

      How can I earn USP Credits?

      USP Credits are earned specifically through purchases made on USP. The more you shop on USP, the more USP Credits you'll collect!

      How can I earn Loyalty Points?

      Earn points by shopping on USP, shopping on Marketplace, or completing tasks in the Earn section.
